Bin Li (李斌)

I am a Lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2021. My research areas are mechanism design and auction theory.

    • Research

    I am currently working on diffusion auction design, which is initialized by us in 2016. The main objective of this direction is to incentivize self-interested agents to share auction information to their competitors via, for example, online social networks. We not only provide necessary theoretical basis for this direction, but also propose novel auctions that dominate classic traditional auctions in terms of both the allocation efficiency and the revenue. Our works have published in many top jorunals and conferences on artificial intelligence and multi-agent system, including AIJ, AAAI, IJCAI, AAMAS, etc.
